Comments 2019-12-09 dive 4, Rashdoo Madivaru Very early wakeup at 5:30 to go again to Rashdoo Madivaru so that we might see hammerheads. So at 6:17 started dive and dropped into the deep blue and stayed around 20 meters for 20 minutes, but hammerheads did not show up. There was no current. Then headed back to the slope and drifted with the mild current. Some reef sharks could be seen coming from the deep. Usual smaller fishes, oriental sweetlips. Me and Nick continued a full hour with the guide and then back up to dhoni. Then breakfast. During breakfast the boat started moving south.
