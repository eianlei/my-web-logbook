Comments Dive 5/8 of the Utö 2020 camp. Thursday was the penultimate diving day at the camp. In the morning we did a dive to Park Victory. Weather was now nice, very light wind and low waves. Now I finally took my camera with me. Olympus TG-6 in PT-058, AOI UWL-4 lens. BGNing tray, attached on each side a Nanight video light attached to tray with two pieces of BGNing 8" carbon fiber float arms. I dove with Leppis, and as he is very familiar with the wreck inside-out, we did a lot of penetrations. Now I mostly shot video. We dove through all the holds, and then visited the engine hall. We had to pay close attendtion to dive compter No-Deco time, as were were diving on air and going quite deep a lot of the time. So diving time was constrained by avoiding deco. It was a nice dive and felt almost too short. I did get some nice video footage, but some of it ruined by the beam of my maing light cutting across the frame. So for next dive better turn off the dive light and only use the video lights.
