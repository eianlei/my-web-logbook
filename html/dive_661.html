Comments 3rd dive on the diving club Kupla annual camp at Utö. 2nd dive to the wreck of Park Victory now in the morning. Still quite rough weather. We did almost similar dive as yesterday, but now went all the way to the tip of the bow and took pictures there. Then dived to the center mast between cargo holds 4 and 5, where depth was 22 meters. Visibility was still very good, but at 15 meters there was a sharp thermocline and below that it was really cold. At 22 meters is was 4 degrees. My drysuit was leaking somewhere around chest, and that did not help with the cold. There would have been enough gas to continue a bit longer, but I was too cold to continue, so we returned to surface. At surface rough weather, but we had to wait for some more divers to return. Then drove Hinkki back to island. Back at island noticed that my suit had leaked a lot, a few liters of sea water inside. I suspected the suit inflation connection which I opened and attached more firmly.
