Comments Third and last dive of the Hanko camp. We were now 2+3 divers, Tero was captain and not diving. I dived with Camilla. This is a very shallow wreck, bottom at only 6 m and some parts above surface. It was called Asa Finn and sank 1957 carrying a cargo of coal. The nickname "Pirtu Veera" comes from the prohibition times in 1930's when the ship was used to smuggle alcohol from Estonia. Now the wreck is badly broken by ice and covered with sea weeds etc. Me and Camilla went around it twice and then we had seen it all. Then we returned to island and started to pack up the camp. Three transports were needed to get all gear to mainland. Me and Tero drove Hinkki boat to Predium harbour. Camilla drove my car to Predium, and Anna drove Tero's car. At Predium me and Camilla had a dinner at my mom's summer cottage. After that I drove home.
