Comments 2. 2021-06-16 D0808 Plus 2nd dive of the trip to the wreck of Plus on Wednesday afternoon. I had dived this many times before. There is a fixed buoy at the site. Plus was a 71 m long iron sailing ship with 3 masts. It sank in storm 14th December 1933 on route to Mariehamn. The ship lies at a depth of 17 to 36 m and is still in good shape. I dived with Rupert using D10 tank 28% Nitrox and my camera. Visibility was pretty good on this dive. We dived to the bow and back. Rupert’s computer showed some deco, so we did a 8 min stop at 5 m on ascent. Lot of decent photos taken. After the dive Orbiit headed south so that we could dive Balder the next day.
