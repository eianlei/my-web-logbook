Comments 2nd dive at Dubrovnik in the afternoon at 2 pm. After the 1st dive went up to the Palace hotel terrace restarant to have lunch. It was a nice restaurant and lunch was really good. Now there was a different group, but still 4 customers, the main guide Vlaho and assistant Andro. This site was just a 10 minute boat ride to south, past the old town is the island of Lokrum. We anchored to the SW side where the is a public beach. Dived along the wall to the cavern that goes to the "Dead Sea" pool inside the island. Amazing +20m visibility again and the cavern was really cool. I really liked this dive. The camera worked again pretty good, but I forgot to check the dome shade alignment, and the shade was in wrong position for most of the dive, so I got this bad vignetting at corners on many pictures. In the cavern the 2x 120 degree lights did not quite cover the entire field of view so you can see the light patterns on the walls.
