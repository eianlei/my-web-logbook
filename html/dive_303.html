Comments DAY DIVING FOUR: Dive One: Habili Shellal, with very little current, just enough to bring the large schools of big-eye jacks, black and red snappers, rainbow runners and surgeonfish in, as well as fusiliers and lots of sergeant majors on the top, and anthias, of course. The little habilis around the main large one covered densely with soft corals in all colors and also schools of sweetlips, bannerfish, blue-lined yellow snappers, and squirrelfish, absolutely awesome dive! The big-eye jacks circled all around us while descending and 4 large tuna kept passing by as well, and two batfish followed us around. Some anemones in the shallows.... Amazing way to start the day!
