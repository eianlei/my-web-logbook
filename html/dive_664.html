Comments 6th and last dive on the diving club Kupla annual camp at Utö. Original plan was to dive Park Victory at 16:00. Unfortunately, the wind had only picked up, and operating the Hinkki boat at south bay in this wind was complicated. We decided that we will not go to Park V and will do a shore dive instead. There were 6 divers doing the shore, and we decided to dive the area around the south bay where the Hinkki boat was moored. Me and Camilla headed S/E and the back. Bottom was quite shallow here at the south side and we could not and deeper than 7 m. Mostly it was between 3 to 5 meters. Visibility was good, but not that much fish to be seen. My dry suit was still not leaking, but after the dive when taking off the suit the neck seal split and was totally beyond repair. Luckily this was the last dive of the camp so no harm. Just need to glue a new neck seal before I can go diving again. Updated 2025-11-23 Place was Sundskär, south shore
