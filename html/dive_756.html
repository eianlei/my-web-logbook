Comments 2019-12-10 dive 9, Moofushi Kandu After breakfast we did Moofushi kandu. We did see quite a many sharks, but the wall was not so interesting, a lot of dead coral. There was a huge cleaning station where we stopped to look at cleaning station life. Moderate current, so we used reef hooks. At the end of the dive we saw and area of recovering coral growth. Otherwise, not so special dive this time. After the dive I noticed that the wide angle lens had some nasty scratches that were visible in the pictures too. I tried to clean the lens the best I could, but the scratches remain and I do not know if anything can be done to remove them. Made the bold decision to remove all pictures from camera SD-card, as I did have two copies of everything already at laptop and phone.
