Comments 2019-12-10 dive 8, Fish Head (Mushimasmingili) The plan was for 5:45 wake-up and the boat would take us to the famous fish head site. This was the best thila so far, full of fishes, big schools of fishers and a lot of healthy coral. I took lots of pictures and videos. There was so much fish, it was hard to decide what to look. There was of course also a good current. Fantastic dive. After the dive back to boat and breakfast. After the dive I noticed that the camera hosuing had leaked a little, the desiccant was totally wet. The camera was totally ok as it is waterproof itself to 10 meters. I just cleaned the housing from inside, but could not figure out why there had been a leak.
