Comments Saarenmaa diving trip 6-9 September 2018 of Kupla diving Club. Dive #3 of the trip. This was a bio dive to the cliffs of Tagalaht. This time I was diving with Liisa, who had a CCR. The dive was going to be very shallow, so dive time was agreed for 60 min. Me and Liisa circled around the bottom for 60 minutes looking at marine life. I had my camera and took lot of macro pictures. In the end we discovered that we had been inside a pot formation and just circling around it instead of going south as originally planned. Inside the pot the deepest point was 6 meters. Only Mikko and Karo had discovered a way out of the pot by crossing a ridge. It was a relaxed and easy dive, with plenty of marine life to see. Water temperature was 19 deg-C, so almost too warm in dry suit. I had 160 bar left at end of dive. Karo enjoyed this dive a lot. After everyone got up, we go back to Veere harbor and then to Pidula Forell for dinner. After dinner we went to sauna. photos: https://photos.leiman.fi/p79867599
