Comments Tornator I weekend diving trip 14-16 September 2018 of Kupla diving Club. After the lunch we had reached the Riuttasaari island, where there is a new pier. Here we dove the rocky cliff of the island. This time I dove with Mky beacause Tero had decided not to dive. We made a plan to dive first 15 min at 20 m towards south, then turn around and come back at 10 m, then finaly 15 min at 6 m. We dove exactly according to plan. At 20 m you could see a few fishes, but mosly just lot of Hemimysis. At 10 m there were perches, and a few crayfishes. Got some photos. The boat sailed to the beach nearby where we stayed over night.
