Comments Dive Three: Habili Gafaar, St Johns, still with quite strong current coming from south, sat on the south corner for most of the dive, enjoying the sight of the many schooling fish: barracuda, black snappers, clouds of black damsels, red bigeyes, jacks, fusiliers, and lots of tuna chasing small fish around in waves, as well as 1 gray reef shark quite deep, on the way looking for the 3 stonefish, we saw the purple one, not the other two, but it was a very beautiful sight!
