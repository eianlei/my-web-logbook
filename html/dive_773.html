Comments Dive 2/8 of the Utö 2020 camp. None of the captains on the island wanted to take this dive, so I got to be the captain of Hinkki for this dive to Tukkiproomu. We were only me, Katja, Marianne, Ida, Eero, Joonas. We departed at 3 PM, wind was already quite hard and rising. It was only 15 min boat ride to the wreck and we attached Hinkki bow to the railing of the wreck section above surface. Everyone else dived before me and Katja were able to dive. I assigned Eero to be dive marshal while me and Katja dived. Visibility was pretty good for this site, as usually visibility here is shit, now you could see like 5 meters. Me and Katja dover around the entire wreck not doing any penetrations to it. When we had all dived, the winf had picked up and was already 10 m/s fro SW and waves about 1 m. Getting back to lagoon went surprisingly smoothly though. Afterwards I was told that others on the island were a bit worried about us due to the hard wind. But everything went smoothly and there were no problems. Next day we could not dive because weather was too rough.
