Comments 2021-10-03 D0832 Porkkalan hylkypuisto The last dive of the Porkkala camp. We got some visitors from DAN and they were going to dive with us at the wreck park of Porkkala. This was not very far from the harbour and was going tpo be really easy diving. Riku and Kalle agreed to dive with me, I would show the way as I had been here before. We agreed 60 min dive time. I had my D12 filled with 32% Nitrox to 220 bar, which was plenty for this dive, I didn’t even use half of my gas. We went down the fixed buoy. Visibility was really poor. Followed the line to the deeper wreck at 20 meter depth. There the visibility was extremely poor and this wreck is also badly broken. Then along the line to the other wreck that was at 5 to 8 m depth. There the visibility was better and it is a bigger wreck in better shape, a bit more to see. Then following the line back to the ascent line, up and back to Orbiit. We did manage to spend 60 min. Then lunch, salmon soup on the way back to harbour. I moved my gear back to car and drove home.
