Comments Early in the morning at 8 am engines were started and Orbiit headed to the wreck of Belliver. It was only 1 hour sailing away, but the captain had lost the exact coordinates and could not locate the wreck. Finally after 1 hour of searching we spotted the permanent marker buoy and anchored Orbiit nearby. Then started diving. Plan was to dive this wreck twice with lunch in between then head back to Enskär island for the night. I dived both dives with Antti again. The first dive I did with the Canon 7D and got pretty good pics: https://photos.leiman.fi/p656284911 Littel did I know then that these would be the last pictures taken with this camera. Next time I dived with it at Indenburg, it flooded. We managed to go around the entire wreck and also looked the rudder and propellers. Found most of the known artifacts, except the ship's bell.
