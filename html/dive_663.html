Comments 5th dive on the diving club Kupla annual camp at Utö. 3rd dive to Park Victory. Now the freshly glued neck seal was put to test. It worked fine, no leaking at all and I was completely dry after the dive. Also felt pretty warm during the dive, except below 15 meter thermocline where water was 4 degrees. Me and Camilla did again the tour to bow and back to the center mast. Visibility was still very good and you could see 15 to 20 meters. The wreck is so huge that even on the 3rd dive there were still details to be seen. At surface the weather continued to be very rough, wind at +10 m/s and ripping tall waves.
