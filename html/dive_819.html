Comments 13. 2021-06-22 D0819 Park Victory 13th dive of the trip. Tuesday late evening we arrived to Utö and dived immediately the wreck of Park Victory. I had been here so many times, but Rupert never. So we agreed not to go inside, just go around the wreck to rear, to front and then up. We took deco stages and were prepared to do some deco. Visibility was not that great, you could not see from one side to another. A big mast on left side near bridge had collapsed. There was no line from bridge to the front piece, so I had to navigate near bottom to the front piece and back. Although dive computers were showing a few minutes of deco at ascent line, that was all gone after we switched to 50% stages, so did not have to do actual deco. After the dive Orbiit started sailing up north towards Örö, but stopped overnight just 1 hour before reaching Örö.
