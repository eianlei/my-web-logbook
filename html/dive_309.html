Comments Dive Three: Habili Albi, with strong current coming from the south, lots of fish off the little pinnacle in the current, fusiliers and surgeonfish, also a school of about 20 dogtooth tuna, lots of pink faced triggerfish making nests but not attacking yet, a large hawksbill turtle lying on the bottom and then swimming off slowly. Beautiful reef top full with soft corals and anthias, a scorpionfish and heaps of Pteraeolidia ianthina nudibranchs crawling about in the shallows on the sheltered side of the pinnacle.
