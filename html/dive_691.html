Comments 2018-12-28 Dive-2 Palm Mar Wall 13:41, 45 min, 24 meters The wall was just 5 min boat ride from the harbor. This time anchor was dropped on the rocks at 15 meters, and there was no current. Then we dropped down to the sandy bottom at 24 m where we immediately saw rays and big schools of snappers. There were also garden eels, and at the rocky wall some moray eels, crayfish sea snails etc. At the end of the dive we saw big sea turtles. This time I managed to take more pictures. Now the 9 kg of lead felt just right for good buoyancy and I could put some air into the dry suit. Thermal comfort without hood was really good. At the surface it was 22 C and clear blue sky. This dive was 45 min and I finished with 87 bar left. There was no deco and NDL was +99 for most of the dive.
