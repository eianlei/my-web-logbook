Comments 2019-12-09 dive 5, Bathala thila After two hours we reached the Bathala Thila, This was quite pretty and interesting coral formations, some moderate current and quite a lot of fish. Saw a frog fish, some baby sharks in the caverns, anemones and fan corals. After 1 hour of good diving back to dhoni and then for lunch. During lunch the boat continued west to Maaya Fushi where we moored for the night. Before the next dive I changed the o-rings on both regulators and cleaned the tank valves. This helped as the bubbling stopped and on next dive the tanks were finally silent.
