Comments Second dive of the Hanko camp. Sunday morning I woke up at 9 AM since the planned departure to Flying Dutchman was 10 AM. I had a good sleep in the tent and the night was warm. Sunny and warm morning, I made quicky some porridge for breakfast. Many campers had partied late so there were only 6 divers able to go diving. This was also 30 m deep, so not for the P1 divers, only P2 and up. To Hinkki boat we loaded Me, Ville, Olli, Antti, Anna, Heikki. I was to dive with Heikki. Olli was captain. The site is in military area so we had call the zone command center to declare the dive. We found the wreck easily, it shows pretty well in sonar and dropped the shot. We did not anchor as it was too deep for that. Ville and Olli departed first, then me and Heikki followed soon. I had my camera. I took some stills and video. Down at the wreck visibility was 3 meters, usual for this site as the sea floor here is muddy. We toured the wooden wreck a couple of times, but then after 20 min were running out of no-deco time, so had to ascend, but we had had seen the wreck well enough. After Antti and Anna surfaced, we picked up the shot and back to island. The next dive was to follow immediately. I had 120 bar left and Camilla wanted to dive with me, so I stayed in boat to continue to the next dive.
