Comments My 2nd dive on the diving club Kupla annual camp at Utö. First dive to the wreck of Park Victory. I had been here many times before, but Camilla this was the first time. Weather was rough, wind at 10 m/s and waves almost a meter high. Me and Camilla went to water first. We went down the line going to the right side mast behind the bridge. Visibility was amazingly good. We headed toward the bow following the line across the #2 hold where the ship has broken into two parts. For the first time I could see from 16 meters all the way down to 30 meters where the debris lied at the bottom. After 15 minutes we turned back from the bow section and then circled around the bridge section, which is now collapsed and exposed. Then slowly back to surface doing 3 min/5m stop at the line. Dive went fine with no issues. On the surface the weather was still quite rough and wind even picking up. I had my Olympus TG5 camera and took lot of pictures.
