Comments 4th dive on the diving club Kupla annual camp at Utö Due to rough weather, we decided to dive from the shore rather than going to the Park Victory wreck again. I also wanted to check if my dry suit was still leaking. We dove again down the west shore and saw lot of fish. At the end of the dive the suit leaked again a little. I inspected the neck seal after the dive and noticed that glue on it was not holding any more. The seal was already detached a bit from the suit. SO obvious cause for a leak. I managed to find some Casco S9 Super glue, borrowed from Ville. I detached the neck seal completely, removed the old glue and re-glued the seal. Updated 2025-11-23 Place was Sundskär, west wall
