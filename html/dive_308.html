Comments Dive Two: Abu Fandera East Fasma, always a very gorgeous passage, and today with an outgoing current we had the best dive, as this is the nicest way to do this dive, visiting the beautiful soft coral covered pinnacles at the beginning and then meandering throughout the hard coral garden with pillars covered in soft corals as well and many snappers, fusiliers, pompanos, tuna, one white tip reef shark, an EAGLE RAY, and a school of about 10 bumphead parrotfish. At the end saw some Pteraeolidia ianthina nudibranchs, so gorgeous! Updated 2025-11-23 Place was Abu Fandera Fasma, East
