Comments Dive 4/4 of the "Idän ihmeet" or "Wonders of the east" camp of Kupla diving club. Dove as a team with Keni and Emmi. On the way back home we stopped at the city of Imatra. We dove the remains of the hydoelectic power station that was built 1898 and decomissioned 1923. The dam wall was built from wood and was submerged when the new dam of Imatra was built 1929. The 100 meter wide structure still remains under water and is about 4 meters tall. Bottom of the stream is at 7 meters. There were some pikes and crayfish. Water was orange to reddish and visibility only a few meters.
