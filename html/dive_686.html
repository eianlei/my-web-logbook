Comments Tornator I weekend diving trip 14-16 September 2018 of Kupla diving Club. We all slept late. Then boat sailed to the tiny island of Kirjavaluoto where we dived around the island. Me, TT and Vode dove as a team again, with a plan to dive counter-clockwise. On north side there was a steep cliff, but we did not go deeper than 15. Then the bottom started to slope gentler and was mostly mud. After 20 min decided to turn around and dive back towards boat. We eventually at 45 min found the boat and surfaced. There wasn't much to see. After the dive stated sailing back towards Kallionimei harbour, and having lunch on the way. On the way sailed by the rock paintings of Astuvansalmi, but could not land beacause the pier was full. Go back to Kallioniemi at 3 PM. Then unloaded the boat and drove back home.
