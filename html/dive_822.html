Comments 2021-07-24 D822 Hauensuoli The 2nd dive of the camp was with Hinkki to the channel of Hauensuoli, just 5 min boat ride from our island, next to the pilot station. Here there are 3 small ship wrecks connected with lines. I dived this time with Marianne. As she took her camera, I decided to leave mine to the boat. But that was maybe a mistake because the visibility was really amazing, and I could have taken good photos. We toured all the 3 wrecks in 50 minutes, but could have spent 60 easily. After the dive back to island, and then it was time to do our traditional sauna and pizza tour at the city of Hanko. Vode and Tero had agreed to drive boats back, but they wanted to leave by 9 PM, so the bar tour was really short. At the island we continued to have our own little party and somehow I got to sleep at 5 AM when sun was rising. We had a lot of fun. The Sunday morning dive was due to start 10 AM, but having stayed up for so late, I had to skip the dive, and so only got the 2 Saturday dives out of this camp. In the afternoon we started to take down the camp and got everyone back to Hanko by 4 PM. I got the task of driving the Hinkki boat to Predium harbour, and that took about an hour. Marianne drove my car. From Predium back to Kuplakammio and home. I prepared my diving ger for the next dive due Monday.
