Comments https://en.divelogs.de/dive/2428705 About the Sandviken wreck: It is a small steamship that sank on 10 Septemeber 1876 in a storm. It was coming from Kornstadt in Sweden with a cargo of rye. In the storm the cargo shifted and the ship listed and sank on the spot. Crew of 11 perished with only 1 survivor. The wreck was found 1998. The ship was built in Liverpool 1871 and has a hull of iron and a steam engine at rear. It was 35 meters long, 6 meters wide.
